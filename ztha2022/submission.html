Format

We welcome four types of submissions:

  1. Technical papers with evaluation results
  2. Position papers on directions for research and development
  3. Review papers
  4. Talk abstract (1 page only) that describes the content of an oral presentation

Each technical/position/review paper must be 4-6 pages (not including references). All accepted papers will be invited to present at the workshop.
